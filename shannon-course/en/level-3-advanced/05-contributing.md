# Contributing & Community

## Introduction

Shannon is open source (AGPL-3.0). While direct PRs aren't accepted, there are many ways to participate.

## How to Help

### Bug Reports
Open issues on [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/KeygraphHQ/shannon/issues) with: version, steps to reproduce, expected vs actual results, and logs.

### Feature Suggestions
Ideas and proposals go in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/KeygraphHQ/shannon/discussions).

### Testing
Run Shannon on various vulnerable apps and share results: OWASP Juice Shop, crAPI, DVWA, WebGoat.
